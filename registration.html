Sightseeing

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is full of life, history, and culture. You can explore grand forts like Amber and Nahargarh, and admire the unique architecture of places like Hawa Mahal and City Palace. Jantar Mantar is a cool spot with old astronomical tools, and temples like Birla Mandir and Galta Ji offer peaceful vibes. If you love shopping, head to Johari or Bapu Bazaar for local jewelry, clothes, and handicrafts. For a fun cultural experience, check out Chokhi Dhani or catch a movie at the famous Raj Mandir Cinema. And if you need a break, Jal Mahal and Central Park are perfect for a relaxing walk.
