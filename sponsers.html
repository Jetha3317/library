Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)

DRDO is the research and development arm of India’s Ministry of Defence, committed to enhancing national security through advanced defence technologies. Established in 1958, DRDO has grown into a network of 41 laboratories, focusing on diverse fields such as aeronautics, missiles, electronics, combat vehicles, and radar systems. Its mission is to achieve self-reliance in critical defence technologies and equip the armed forces with state-of-the-art weapon systems and equipment. DRDO’s innovations include strategic missile systems like Agni and Prithvi, the Light Combat Aircraft Tejas, and sophisticated electronic warfare systems, significantly strengthening India’s defence capabilities.
