About the Conference

Jaipur Engineering College and Research Centre (JECRC), Jaipur, in collaboration with JECRC University and the Society for Library Professionals (SLA), is proud to host the 13th International Library Information Professionals conference (ILIPS) – International Conference on Emerging Technologies for Smart Libraries: AI, IoT & Empowerment.

The 13th International Library Information Professionals conference (I-LIPS 2025) is a premier global academic and professional gathering dedicated to exploring the future of libraries, information science, and knowledge management in the digital era. With the theme “Emerging Technologies for Smart Libraries: AI, IoT & Empowerment”, the conference brings together thought leaders, Academicians, Researchers, Professionals, and Innovators to share insights on how disruptive technologies are transforming the library ecosystem.

The conference will be held at JECRC Foundation, Plot No. IS-2036 to 2039, Ramchandrapura Industrial Area, Phase-III, Sitapura, Jaipur – 302022.

This prestigious event serves as a global platform for library and information science (LIS) professionals, academicians, technologists, and researchers to explore how emerging technologies are transforming the future of libraries and information systems.

Theme: Innovation in Libraries

The library and information profession has always been at the forefront of adopting new technologies. With the advent of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), and other smart technologies, libraries are experiencing a paradigm shift in their role, services, and impact. Socioeconomic changes and digital innovations across the globe are influencing how information is created, managed, and delivered. This conference emphasizes the importance of continuous innovation in libraries to remain relevant and effective in meeting the demands of modern learners, researchers, and communities.

Focus Area: Future Information Systems

Libraries and information centers today are facing transformative challenges. Traditional functions are evolving, and professionals must adapt to new tools, digital ecosystems, and user expectations. This international conference aims to:
• Highlight the challenges in developing and managing next-generation information systems.
• Explore cutting-edge solutions that leverage AI, IoT, cloud computing, big data, and smart technologies.
• Encourage collaboration between global experts, professionals, and institutions.
• Redefine the role of LIS professionals in a rapidly changing information landscape.

Who Should Attend

This event is designed to attract a global audience from diverse fields connected to information science and technology. Participants include:
• Library and Information Science (LIS) professionals
• Information scientists and knowledge managers
• Academicians, Researchers, Scientist & Industry Person.
• Technology developers and innovators in digital libraries
• Policy makers and institutional leaders
• Students pursuing LIS and information technology By attending, participants will have the opportunity to exchange knowledge, share best practices, and network with leading experts who are driving innovation in smart libraries worldwide.
