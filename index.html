About the Conference

New Technological developments are very eagerly adopted and applied by the library and information science field. Around the globe, socioeconomic developments have always influenced the LIS profession. As a result of the data economy, artificial intelligence, big data, internet of things and robotics, teaching, learning, research, and industry have undergone drastic changes, and those changes are bound to impact on LIS practices.

The purpose of this international conference is to shed light on the challenges and demands associated with future information systems. Currently, the library and information profession is facing a total role shift. As user engagement demands become more important as well as creative outcomes in academics and research, new services must be offered.

This conference will identify, acquire, and polish with groundbreaking resourcefulness the current and future demands in librarianship and information science.

Who Should Attend: This major academic and professional event anticipates global participation. Information scientists and professionals involved in library research, information resource management, technology application, and other related disciplines in the field of library and information studies are welcome to participate.
