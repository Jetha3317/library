Aim and Objective

This conference aims to explore emerging trends and innovations in Library and Information Science, emphasizing sustainability practices and digital transformation in modern library systems. It seeks to highlight advancements in library education, services, and technologies that support sustainable development goals.

Bringing together library professionals, academicians, researchers, policymakers, and students, the conference provides a global platform for sharing knowledge, discussing challenges, and fostering collaborative solutions. It promotes the dissemination of impactful research and innovative strategies to enhance library effectiveness in the digital era.

Selected papers will be published in reputed journals and conference proceedings indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, Google Scholar, and other academic databases, ensuring international visibility.

Key focus areas include digital libraries, AI in information retrieval, data management, e-governance, user-centric services, and the evolving role of libraries in sustainable development. The conference aims to foster interdisciplinary networking and contribute to the advancement of libraries as inclusive, sustainable knowledge hubs.
