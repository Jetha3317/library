Aim and Objective

This conference aims to explore the recent trends in the Engineering Education Systems & Sustainability using various engineering tools. It addresses innovative advancements in Engineering Trends in Education Systems & Sustainability issues with specific reference to the area of Education Systems & Sustainability.

The conference provides an international platform for researchers, academicians, industry representatives, government officials, students, and other stakeholders in the field to explore opportunities, disseminate, and acquire beneficial knowledge from the discussions on various themes. Selected papers will be published in different proceedings and journals indexed by Scopus, Web of Science, Google Scholar, and others, ensuring international exposure and opportunities for citations.

Additionally, the conference fosters Networking by bringing together stakeholders from various countries to exchange thoughts, ideas, and solutions. This facilitates new connections and collaborations for future research endeavors.

The focus of the conference is on current advances in Engineering research and its role in academic sustainability, covering fields such as Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Mechanical, Electronics, Electrical, Civil, and other related disciplines.
